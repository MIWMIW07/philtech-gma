About PHILTECH

More Than A Decade of Global Success Through Academic Excellence

Our History

Philippine Technological Institute of Science Arts and Trade Inc., founded in 2010 as a non-stock non-profit non-sectarian private Educational Institution to blaze the trail in the field of technical education.

With every member of the Board of Trustees going out of their way to promote the school and its program offerings, PHILTECH has grown from a single branch in Tanay, Rizal to multiple locations serving over 1,500 students.

By 2021, the GMA branch offered its first bachelor's program, Bachelor in Technical Vocational Teacher Education. The institution continued to expand with Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Bachelor of Science in Office Administration programs.

Today, PHILTECH continues its mission to provide global success through academic excellence with admiration to knowledge and appreciation to skills.
