About PHILTECH

PHILTECH is a premier educational institution committed to providing quality education that combines technological expertise with arts and trade skills.

Our Story

Established with a vision to revolutionize education in the Philippines, PHILTECH has been at the forefront of combining technical education with arts and trade skills for over two decades.

Our institution has grown from a small technical school to a comprehensive educational institution offering programs from senior high school to college degrees.
