Our Mission in General Mariano Alvarez

For over 50 years, Philtech GMA has been dedicated to nurturing young minds and developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to make a positive impact in their communities and the world.

We believe in fostering creativity, critical thinking, and character development through innovative teaching methods and a supportive learning environment that celebrates diversity and encourages personal growth.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond academics to include leadership development, community service, and global citizenship preparation for students in Cavite and surrounding areas.
